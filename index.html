To Download:

1. Visit this page on your Android device.

2. Enable third-party app installs on your device.

Android 2.3 and earlier Settings->Applications->Unknown Sources

Android 4.0+ Settings->Security->Unknown Sources

  • Basic Pub/Sub
  • Channels for chat rooms
  • Presence
  • Push Notifications
  • History and playback on channels
